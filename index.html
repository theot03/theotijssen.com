About Me

My name is Theo Tijssen and I am an enthusiastic software engineer. I'm currently the Senior Application Developer at MPX. Working mainly with PHP and MySQL, I develop custom document composition applications for a broad range of industries that include Energy, Healthcare, Insurance, Telecommunications and Financial Services. I have a proven track record in both development as well as project management, while also exceeding expectations.

I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering, with a focus in Computer Engineering, from the University of Southern Maine in 2013. While at USM, I served on the Engineering Student Committee and helped with many initiatives that included funding and student activities. Some relevant courses included Data Structurs, Object Oriented Design, Microprocessor Systems, Differential Equations, Circuits and Electronics.
